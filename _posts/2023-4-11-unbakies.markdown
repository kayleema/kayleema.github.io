## Ingredients
* **Sugar** 1.75cups = 414ml
* **Butter** 0.5cup = 113g
* **Milk** 0.5cup = 118ml
* **Cocoa** 4tbsp = 59ml
* **Peanut butter** 0.5cups = 125g = 118ml
* **Quick Oats** 3cups = 710ml
* **Vanilla** 1tsp = 5ml

## Preparation
* *Boil* **sugar, butter, milk,and cocoa** **1.5min**
* **Remove** from heat
* Add **everything else**
* Drop **spoonfulls on parchment**
* Let *cool* until solidified
* **Done!**
